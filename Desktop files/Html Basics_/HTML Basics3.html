How to start your career in a digital startup

Identify your interests and skills first, Think about the areas of digital startups that interest you, and what skills you can bring to the table. For example, if you're passionate about marketing and have experience in social media, you might consider a role in digital marketing.

Importance of teamwork in the project

Teamwork is essential for the success of any project. When people work together on a project, they bring different perspectives and ideas to the table. This can lead to better problem-solving and creative solutions.

Manage you finances from now on for future purposes

Think about what you want to achieve in the short-term and long-term, such as saving for a down payment on a house or building a retirement fund. Set specific, measurable goals and track your progress towards achieving them.
